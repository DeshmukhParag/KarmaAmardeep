About Us

Educational Inequality is a multi-faceted problem. A lot of efforts have been undertaken to address the issue of attendance and skill development. But, one of the key contributing factors is infrastructural imparity, especially in socioeconomically challenged parts. We are aiming to address the problem of lack of electricity in rural areas with a simple yet effective solution- Amardeep- A lamp that will last a lifetime.

Our Story

We decided to work for a world where there will be a level playing field. Any child with passion, dedication and dreams won’t be left behind because of our failure to support them.

After understanding the issue, we went ahead & developed our own solution- Amardeep- A lamp that runs on solar energy. Education is a fundamental right. Many of our finest NGOs, NPOs, and municipalities have fought to provide our children with a solid education. But what happens when the students go home? Did you know that 50 million households in India do not have access to electricity? (Source: economic times) These students living don’t get the opportunity to self-study and practice their schoolwork at home at night. The students who do, currently use harmful kerosene lamps.

Our Mission

Our children are our future and it is our responsibility to nurture their potential. Our mission is to build a platform where anyone can work for children and watch their small step bring big tides of change.

Millions of children in remote & impoverished villages don’t have the basic amenities and opportunities we often take for granted, but their hunger for success is fierce. Together, we can light up their potential and help them become the shining stars they intrinsically are. The lamp equates to 3 extra hours of studying a night. Let’s say the student studies 5 days a week. That’s 15 hours of extra studying per week. Extend that to a year, and that is 780 hours of excellently lighted studying. This can make a huge difference in children’s academic success, opening up new doors of opportunity for them.
